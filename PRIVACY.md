# Privacy Policy for QRinajar

Last updated: July 19, 2026

QRinajar is designed to work entirely offline. This policy explains, simply, what that means for your data.

## What we collect

Nothing. QRinajar does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no servers this app talks to.

## How your data is used

Everything you enter into QRinajar (website URLs, Wi-Fi credentials, contact details, text, images you use as a logo, saved presets) stays on your device, in the app's local storage. None of it is transmitted anywhere.

## Camera

QRinajar's QR scanner uses your device's camera solely to scan a QR code you point it at. The camera feed is processed locally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. Camera access is requested only when you tap the scan button, and only after you're shown an explanation of why it's needed.

## Photos

QRinajar can save the QR codes you create to your Photos library. This uses add-only access — the app can save images to your library but cannot read, browse, or access any other photos on your device.

## Third-party services

QRinajar does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's privacy

QRinajar does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
